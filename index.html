ABOUT ME

My name is Anika Rao Srinivasan! I'm a second year student at the
University of Waterloo, pursuing my undergraduate degree in Global
Business and Digital Arts. In my free time, I engage in a number of
hobbies and creative endeavours, including traditional and digital
arts, crochet sewing, and more. My design style can vary a lot
depending on my inspirations and moods. I tried to convey both sides
of my design through this interactive webpage.

This webpage is a curated display of some of my creative projects.
You may use the toggle switch in the upper right corner to swap
between "Day Mode" and "Night Mode". Each mode will showcase
different projects. "Day Mode" is a collection of my brighter pieces,
displaying a colourful, maximalist aesthetic. "Night Mode", on the
other hand, shows a darker side with celestial themes.
